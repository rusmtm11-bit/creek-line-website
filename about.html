About Creek Line Limited

Creek Line Limited is a Hong Kong-originated commodity trading company engaged in the international trade and supply of Oil & Gas products, metals, minerals, and industrial commodities. The company operates with a global commercial approach, supporting international procurement, supplier coordination, and cross-border trade operations across multiple markets.

Our objective is to establish long-term and mutually beneficial partnerships through reliable service, operational efficiency, and professional business conduct.

Our Mission

To provide reliable and efficient commodity trading solutions through responsible business practices, international cooperation, and strong operational coordination.

Our Vision

To become a trusted international commodity trading partner recognized for professionalism, reliability, and long-term commercial value.
